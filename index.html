Space Apps Pasadena 2021

The NASA International Space Apps Challenge (Space Apps) is an international hackathon for students of all levels in cities around the world, where teams use NASA's free and open data to address real-world problems on Earth and in space.
On October 2-3, participants will form teams of 2-6 and solve challenges submitted by NASA personnel over a 48-hour period. Project submissions are judged by space agency experts and winners are selected for one of 10 Global Awards! Learn more on the International Space Apps Challenge site.

Meet The Team

Space Apps Pasadena is hosted by the She.Codes club at Pasadena City College! She.Codes seek to bridge the gender gap in STEM, with an emphasis on Computer Science and Engineering.
We support and encourage our fellow students in engineering, providing a sense of community, work to dismantle the untrue stereotype that engineering is not a career for women, and advocate to have women more equally represented in the field of technology.
